<p><p>Tilt switches are devices that can detect the change in orientation or angle of an object. There are two main types of tilt switches in the market: mercury-free tilt switches and mercury tilt switches. Mercury-free tilt switches use alternative technologies such as ball bearings or conductive liquids to detect tilt, making them more environmentally friendly. On the other hand, mercury tilt switches use mercury as a conductive element to sense the tilt. Due to environmental concerns, mercury-free tilt switches are becoming more popular in the market.</p></p>
