<p><p>Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) and Cyclic Olefin Polymer (COP) are types of thermoplastic polymers widely used in various industries due to their high transparency, chemical resistance, and low water absorption. COC is a copolymer of ethylene and norbornene, while COP is a homopolymer of norbornene. Both materials are commonly used in packaging, healthcare, and optical applications. COC has better optical properties and is commonly used in medical devices, while COP is favored for applications requiring higher heat resistance and chemical stability.</p></p>
