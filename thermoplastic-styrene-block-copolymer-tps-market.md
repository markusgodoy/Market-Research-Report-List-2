<p><p>Thermoplastic Styrene Block Copolymer (TPS) is divided into various types based on the different combinations of styrene and diene monomers. Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS) is known for its high strength and flexibility, while Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS) offers excellent elasticity. SEBS combines the properties of both SBS and SIS, making it versatile for various applications. SEPS provides good chemical resistance and heat stability. Other TPS types offer unique characteristics suitable for specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
