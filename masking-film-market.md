<p><p>Masking film is categorized into two main types based on temperature resistance: High Temperature Resistance Type and Normal Temperature Type. The High Temperature Resistance Type is designed to withstand high heat levels, making it suitable for applications in industries like automotive or aerospace where extreme temperatures are involved. On the other hand, the Normal Temperature Type is used for more general masking applications where high heat resistance is not required. Both types of masking film offer protection from paint, dust, and debris during various finishing tasks.</p></p>
