<p><p>The condensate drain valve market can be divided into three types based on operation: fully automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully automatic valves are equipped with sensors to detect moisture levels and automatically release condensate. Semi-automatic valves require some manual intervention, such as adjusting settings or pressing a button to release condensate. Manual valves rely entirely on manual operation, requiring the user to regularly check and release condensate. Each type offers different levels of automation and convenience for managing condensate drainage.</p></p>
