<p><strong>The Pumped Hydro Storage Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed-loop System</li><li>Open-loop System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pumped hydro storage systems can be classified into closed-loop and open-loop systems. In a closed-loop system, water is circulated between two reservoirs to generate power during peak demand periods. On the other hand, in an open-loop system, water is drawn from external water sources such as rivers or lakes for power generation. Other market types include variable-speed systems and advanced hybrid systems, which combine pumped hydro storage with other renewable energy sources for more efficient energy storage and generation.</p></p>
