Wi-Fi controllers allow users to monitor and control their barbecue temperature remotely through an app on their smartphone or tablet. Bluetooth controllers offer a more limited range but still allow for convenient monitoring and control. Wired controllers are connected directly to the grill and provide a reliable, hands-on approach to temperature regulation. Each type offers its own unique benefits and features to cater to different user preferences.</p></p>
