<p><strong>The RTD Thermowells Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Thermowells</li><li>Flanged Thermowells</li><li>Socket Weld Thermowells</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RTD thermowells are commonly categorized into four market types based on their mounting methods: Threaded thermowells, Flanged thermowells, Socket Weld thermowells, and Others. Threaded thermowells are typically screwed into the process equipment, while Flanged thermowells are connected to the process equipment using flanges. Socket Weld thermowells are welded directly to the process equipment. The "Others" category includes alternative mounting methods such as weld-in or sanitary connections. Each market type offers unique advantages and is selected based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
