<p><p>The electronic alarm clock market includes two main types: LED and LCD clocks. LED clocks utilize light-emitting diodes to display the time in a bright and clear manner. These clocks are known for their energy efficiency and long lifespan. On the other hand, LCD clocks use liquid crystal displays to show the time and typically have a sleek and modern design. LED clocks are popular for their simplicity and readability, while LCD clocks are favored for their versatility and customization options.</p></p>
