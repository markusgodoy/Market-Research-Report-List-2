<p><p>Fast recovery diodes are commonly available in two market types: single common anode and dual common anode. In a single common anode configuration, multiple diodes share a common anode terminal, allowing for simpler circuit design and cost-effective solutions. On the other hand, dual common anode diodes incorporate two anode terminals, enabling more flexible applications and circuit configurations. Both types offer fast recovery times and high efficiency, making them ideal for use in power electronics and switching applications.</p></p>
