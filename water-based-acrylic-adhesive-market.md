<p><p>Water-based acrylic adhesives can be categorized into different types based on their ionic charges. Anionic type adhesives have negatively charged particles, which allow them to bond well with porous substrates. Cationic type adhesives, on the other hand, have positively charged particles, making them suitable for bonding non-porous materials. Other types of water-based acrylic adhesives may include hybrids or specialty formulations. These different types cater to various bonding requirements and substrates, providing a wide range of options for different applications.</p></p>
