<p><p>Substrates for HDDs are essential components in the manufacturing of hard disk drives. Two common types are glass substrates and aluminum substrates. Glass substrates are favored for their durability, heat resistance, and high data storage capacity, making them suitable for high-performance HDDs. On the other hand, aluminum substrates are known for their low cost and lightweight properties, making them a popular choice for low to mid-range HDDs. Both types have their advantages and are used in different segments of the HDD market.</p></p>
