<p><p>Magnesium market types include Magnesite, Dolomite, and Carnallite. Magnesite is a mineral primarily composed of magnesium carbonate, used in the production of refractory materials and magnesium compounds. Dolomite is a calcium-magnesium carbonate mineral found in sedimentary rocks, used in agriculture, construction, and steel production. Carnallite is a mineral containing magnesium, potassium, and chloride, used in fertilizer production. Each of these market types plays a significant role in various industries, contributing to the overall demand for magnesium products.</p></p>
