<p><p>Cementitious Waterproofing techniques involve using a mixture of cement and additives to create a waterproof barrier. Liquid Waterproofing Membrane is applied as a thin layer of liquid coating that solidifies to form a waterproof seal. Bituminous Membrane consists of layers of bitumen-based sheets that are heated and melted onto surfaces for waterproofing. Bituminous Coating is a solvent-based protective coating applied to surfaces for waterproofing. Polyurethane Liquid Membrane is a versatile, seamless coating that provides strong waterproofing protection for a variety of surfaces.</p></p>
