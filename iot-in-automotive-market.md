There are three main market types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. Embedded systems are built into the vehicle's hardware, providing continuous connectivity. Tethered systems rely on external devices like smartphones for connectivity. Integrated systems offer seamless connectivity within the vehicle's infotainment system. Each market type offers unique benefits and plays a crucial role in the advancement of IoT technology in the automotive industry.</p></p>
