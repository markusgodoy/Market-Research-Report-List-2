<p><p>Air to Air Heat Exchangers market can be classified into two main types: Plate Type Air Heat Exchangers and Tube Type Air Heat Exchangers. Plate Type Air Heat Exchangers consist of a series of plates with space in between for air to pass through, allowing for efficient heat transfer. On the other hand, Tube Type Air Heat Exchangers use a series of tubes to transfer heat between two air streams. Both types are commonly used in HVAC systems to improve energy efficiency and indoor air quality.</p></p>
