<p><p>Structural hollow sections refer to steel products with hollow shapes that are used in various construction applications. There are two main types of structural hollow sections in the market: hot formed type and cold formed type. Hot formed sections are produced by heating steel to high temperatures and shaping it into the desired form, while cold formed sections are created by bending or folding steel sheets without the use of heat. Both types are widely used in building structures, infrastructure projects, and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
