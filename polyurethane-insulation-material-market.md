<p><p>Polyurethane insulation materials are widely used in the market in three main types: flexible foam, rigid foam, and spray foam. </p><p>Flexible foam is commonly used for cushioning and packaging applications due to its ability to easily conform to various shapes. Rigid foam is a popular choice for insulation in buildings and appliances, as it provides high thermal resistance. </p><p>Spray foam is applied as a liquid, expanding to create a seamless insulating barrier, making it ideal for hard-to-reach areas. Each type offers unique properties suited to different applications in the market.</p></p>
