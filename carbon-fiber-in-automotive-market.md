<p><p>Carbon fiber is extensively used in the automotive industry in various forms such as Sheet Molding Compound (SMC), Short Fiber Thermoplastic (SFT), Long Fiber Thermoplastic (LFT), Prepreg, and others. SMC involves reinforcing fibers in a thermosetting resin, while SFT and LFT use short and long carbon fibers in a thermoplastic matrix respectively. Prepreg consists of pre-impregnated carbon fibers with resin. These different types offer lightweight, high-strength material options for automotive parts, contributing to improved performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
