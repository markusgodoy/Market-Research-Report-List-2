<p><p>T-Slot Cutters are a type of tool used in machining to create slots or grooves in materials. There are two common types of T-Slot Cutters based on their shank design - Taper Shank and Straight Handle. Taper Shank T-Slot Cutters have a tapered shank that fits into a matching tapered hole in the spindle, providing a secure connection. Straight Handle T-Slot Cutters have a straight shank that can be held in a collet or chuck. Both types are used in various industries for cutting precise and accurate slots in materials.</p></p>
