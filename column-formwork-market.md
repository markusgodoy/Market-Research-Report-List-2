<p><p>Column formwork in the construction industry comes in two main types - all steel structure and wooden steel structure. All steel structure formwork is made entirely of steel, offering high durability and strength. On the other hand, wooden steel structure formwork combines the natural properties of wood with the strength of steel. Both types of formwork provide reliable support for columns during the pouring of concrete, ensuring that the structure remains strong and stable.</p></p>
