<p><p>UHF Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) inlay market consists of two main types: UHF dry inlay and UHF wet inlay. UHF dry inlays are made of a dry adhesive material and are typically used for applications where flexibility and thinness are crucial. On the other hand, UHF wet inlays are made of a wet adhesive material and are commonly used in applications where durability and moisture resistance are important. Both types of inlays are essential components of RFID systems for tracking and identifying objects in various industries.</p></p>
