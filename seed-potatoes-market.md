<p><strong>The Seed Potatoes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Seed Potatoes</li><li>Micro Propagation Seed Potatoes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conventional Seed Potatoes refer to potato seeds that are grown through traditional methods, such as cutting larger potatoes into smaller pieces for planting. These seeds are commonly used by farmers for cultivating potato crops. Micro Propagation Seed Potatoes, on the other hand, are produced through tissue culture techniques in a controlled environment. This method allows for the rapid multiplication of disease-free potato seeds. Both types of seed potatoes play a crucial role in the agricultural industry by ensuring high-quality potato production.</p></p>
