<p><ul><li>Assembly (On Frame)</li><li>Terminal and Distribution Strips (No Frame)</li><li>Powered (On Frame)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solderless breadboards come in different market types based on their design and features. Assembly (On Frame) breadboards have a frame around the board for easier handling and organization. Terminal and Distribution Strips (No Frame) breadboards do not have a frame, allowing for more flexibility in connecting components. Powered (On Frame) breadboards have a built-in power source on the frame, making it convenient for projects that require power. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the electronics and prototyping industry.</p></p>
