<p><strong>The LED Lighting Driver ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED Lighting Driver ICs are available in two main types: SMD Type and Through Hole Type. SMD (Surface Mount Device) Type ICs are small and lightweight, making them ideal for compact and modern lighting fixtures. On the other hand, Through Hole Type ICs have leads that are inserted into holes on the circuit board, providing a more secure and stable connection. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the lighting application, such as size constraints and ease of installation.</p></p>
