<p><p>The percutaneous mechanical circulatory support devices market consists of various types of devices, including Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) devices, Extracorporeal Ventricular Assist Devices (EVAD), and others. ECMO devices provide temporary heart and lung support outside of the body, while EVADs are used to support the failing heart. Other devices in this market may include percutaneous ventricular assist devices. These devices are crucial in providing mechanical support to patients with severe cardiac or respiratory failure, helping to maintain circulation and oxygenation.</p></p>
