<p><p>Fluoro Polymer market consists of different types of materials such as PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PVDF (Polyvinylidene fluoride), Fluoroelastomers, and other variations. PTFE is widely used for its heat resistance and low friction properties. PVDF is known for its chemical resistance and durability. Fluoroelastomers offer high temperature resistance and chemical inertness. Other types of Fluoro Polymers include FEP (Fluorinated ethylene propylene) and ETFE (Ethylene tetrafluoroethylene), each with unique properties catering to specific industries and applications.</p></p>
