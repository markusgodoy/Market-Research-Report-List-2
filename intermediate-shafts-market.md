<p><ul><li>Slidable</li><li>Collapsible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intermediate shafts in the automotive industry come in two main types: slidable and collapsible. Slidable intermediate shafts have the ability to adjust their length, allowing for flexibility in different vehicle designs. Meanwhile, collapsible intermediate shafts are designed to collapse in the event of a collision, absorbing impact and reducing the risk of injury to passengers. Both types of intermediate shafts play a crucial role in providing smooth and efficient power transmission in vehicles while also ensuring safety in the event of unexpected events.</p></p>
