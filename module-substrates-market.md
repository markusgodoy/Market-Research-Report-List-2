<p><ul><li>Thin Film Substrate</li><li>Thick Film Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Module Substrates market consists of two main types: Thin Film Substrates and Thick Film Substrates. Thin Film Substrates are made by depositing a thin layer of material onto a base substrate, providing excellent electrical performance and high precision. Thick Film Substrates, on the other hand, are created by printing a thick layer of conductive material onto a substrate, offering good thermal conductivity and high power handling capabilities. Both types of substrates are essential components in the manufacturing of electronic modules for various industries.</p></p>
