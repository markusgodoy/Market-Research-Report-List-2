<p><strong>The Sound-proof Cabinets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Type</li><li>Partially Closed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed type sound-proof cabinets are fully enclosed cabinets with sound-proofing material on all sides to block out external noise completely. These cabinets are ideal for environments where absolute silence is required, such as recording studios or laboratories. Partially closed type cabinets, on the other hand, have sound-proofing material on some sides but may have openings or vents for airflow. These cabinets are suitable for environments where some noise reduction is needed without completely isolating the equipment inside.</p></p>
