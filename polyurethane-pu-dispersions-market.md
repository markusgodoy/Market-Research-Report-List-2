<p><p>Polyurethane (PU) dispersions are available in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based PU dispersions are environmentally friendly and offer low VOC emissions, making them suitable for indoor applications. On the other hand, solvent-based PU dispersions provide excellent chemical resistance and durability, making them ideal for outdoor use. Both types have their own unique characteristics and advantages, catering to different market needs and applications.</p></p>
