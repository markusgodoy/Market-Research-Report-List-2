<p><p>Linerless paper is used in various markets including Direct Thermal, Thermal Transfer, and Laser printing. Direct Thermal linerless paper is heat sensitive and does not require ribbon or ink for printing, commonly used in applications like shipping labels. Thermal Transfer linerless paper requires a ribbon for printing high-quality and durable labels, suitable for industries like healthcare. Laser linerless paper is compatible with laser printers and is commonly used for printing receipts and tickets. Each type offers unique benefits and is used in different industries depending on the printing requirements.</p></p>
