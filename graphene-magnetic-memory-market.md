<p><p>Graphene magnetic memory market types include chemical vapor deposition (CVD), graphite ore, synthesis on SiC, Scotch tape method, and others. CVD involves growing graphene on a substrate through chemical reactions. Graphite ore is extracted from natural sources and converted into graphene. Synthesis on SiC involves depositing graphene on silicon carbide substrates. The Scotch tape method involves peeling off layers of graphene from graphite using tape. Other methods may include laser techniques or using different types of substrates to create graphene for magnetic memory applications.</p></p>
