Long-range PCR is used for amplifying long DNA fragments. MSP is specifically designed to detect DNA methylation patterns. Fast-cycling PCR reduces the overall run time of the PCR reaction. Other types of PCR workstations include those designed for specific research applications or customized workflows.</p></p>
