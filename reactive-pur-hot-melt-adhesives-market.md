<p><p>Reactive PUR Hot-melt adhesives are classified into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I adhesives are typically used for bonding materials with high heat resistance requirements, such as automotive components or furniture. They offer excellent strength and durability even at high temperatures. Type II adhesives, on the other hand, are more flexible and are often used for applications where there may be slight movement or flexibility required in the bonded materials, such as in the assembly of electronics or packaging materials.</p></p>
