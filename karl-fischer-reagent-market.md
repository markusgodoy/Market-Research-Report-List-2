<p><ul><li>Volumetric Reagent</li><li>Coulometric Reagent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Karl Fischer reagent is used for measuring trace amounts of water in various industrial products. There are two main types of Karl Fischer reagents: volumetric reagents and coulometric reagents. Volumetric reagents are used to titrate water in samples and measure the amount of reagent needed to reach the endpoint. Coulometric reagents generate iodine electrochemically to react with water in the sample, providing a more accurate measurement of water content. Both types of reagents play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and reliability of products in industries such as pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, and food manufacturing.</p></p>
