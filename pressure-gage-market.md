<p><p>Pressure gages are tools used to measure the pressure within a closed system. There are three main types of pressure gages: Absolute Pressure Type, which measures pressure relative to a perfect vacuum; Gauge Pressure Type, which measures pressure relative to atmospheric pressure; and Vacuum Type, which measures pressures below atmospheric pressure. Each type serves a specific purpose in various industries, such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, where accurate pressure measurements are crucial for safe and efficient operations.</p></p>
