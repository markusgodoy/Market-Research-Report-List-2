<p><p>Flour applicators come in two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic flour applicators are fully automated systems that require minimal human intervention. They are designed for high-volume production and ensure consistent and precise application of flour. On the other hand, semi-automatic flour applicators require some manual input but still offer efficiency and precision in flour application. They are suitable for smaller-scale operations or those that require more flexibility and control in the flour application process. Both types play a vital role in enhancing the efficiency and quality of baked goods production.</p></p>
