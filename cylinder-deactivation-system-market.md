<p><p>Cylinder deactivation systems can be classified into three main categories based on the number of cylinders they deactivate: 4-cylinder deactivation systems, 6-cylinder deactivation systems, and above 6-cylinder deactivation systems. The 4-cylinder systems typically deactivate two out of four cylinders, while the 6-cylinder systems deactivate three out of six cylinders. Above 6-cylinder systems can deactivate varying numbers of cylinders depending on the engine size. Each type of system offers different levels of fuel efficiency and performance improvements, catering to a wide range of vehicle applications.</p></p>
