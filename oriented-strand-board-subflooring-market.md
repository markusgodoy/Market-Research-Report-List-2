<p><p>Oriented Strand Board (OSB) subflooring is classified into four different market types: OSB/1, OSB/2, OSB/3, and OSB/4. OSB/1 is used for general-purpose applications where structural integrity is not a top priority. OSB/2 is suitable for load-bearing applications in dry conditions. OSB/3 is recommended for load-bearing applications in humid conditions. OSB/4 is designed for heavy-duty load-bearing applications such as industrial flooring. Each market type has specific characteristics and performance capabilities to meet different requirements in construction projects.</p></p>
