<p><p>Foamed concrete is categorized into three main types based on its density: low density, medium density, and high density. Low density foamed concrete typically has a density ranging from 300 to 800 kg/m3, making it suitable for insulation and filling applications. Medium density foamed concrete, with a density of 800 to 1600 kg/m3, is often used for structural and non-structural applications. High density foamed concrete, with a density above 1600 kg/m3, is commonly utilized in structural and load-bearing applications where strength and durability are key requirements.</p></p>
