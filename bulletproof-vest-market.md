<p><p>Bulletproof vests are available in three main types: soft, hard, and composite. Soft bulletproof vests are made of flexible materials like Kevlar, providing protection against small arms fire. Hard bulletproof vests are constructed with rigid plates of ceramic or metallic materials to stop high-velocity bullets. Composite bulletproof vests combine the benefits of both soft and hard vests, using a mix of materials like Kevlar and ceramic plates. Each type serves a specific purpose depending on the level of protection needed in different situations.</p></p>
