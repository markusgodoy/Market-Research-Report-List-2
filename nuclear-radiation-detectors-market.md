<p><p>Nuclear radiation detectors are classified into three main types: gas ionization detectors, semiconductor detectors, and scintillation detectors. Gas ionization detectors utilize a gas-filled chamber to detect radiation. Semiconductor detectors use semiconductor materials to convert radiation into electrical signals. Scintillation detectors use scintillating materials to produce light when exposed to radiation. Each type of detector has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as healthcare, nuclear energy, and environmental monitoring. The nuclear radiation detectors market continues to grow due to the increasing demand for accurate and reliable radiation detection technology.</p></p>
