<p><strong>The Automatic Dependent Surveillance Broadcast (ADS-B) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ADS-B Out</li><li>ADS-B In</li><li>ADS-B Ground Stations</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Dependent Surveillance Broadcast (ADS-B) is a surveillance technology used in aviation to track aircraft. ADS-B Out refers to the capability of an aircraft to broadcast its own position and other data to ground stations and other aircraft. ADS-B In allows aircraft to receive information from other aircraft and ground stations. Ground stations receive and relay data from aircraft equipped with ADS-B Out. The ADS-B Ground Stations market involves the infrastructure used to collect and disseminate ADS-B data for air traffic control and other purposes.</p></p>
