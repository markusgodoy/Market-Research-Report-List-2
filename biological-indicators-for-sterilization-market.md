Self-contained biological indicators are complete systems containing a spore strip and culture medium, providing a clear and instant result of sterilization effectiveness. Biological indicator strips are small paper strips coated with spores used to monitor sterilization processes. They are easy to use and can be quickly analyzed in a laboratory setting. Both types of indicators are crucial in ensuring the effectiveness of sterilization processes in various industries such as healthcare and food manufacturing.</p></p>
