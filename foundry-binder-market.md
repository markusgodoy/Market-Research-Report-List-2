<p><ul><li>Organic Binder</li><li>Inorganic Binder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foundry binder market can be segmented into two main types: organic binder and inorganic binder. Organic binders are derived from natural sources such as plant-based materials and synthetic polymers, offering good strength and flexibility. In contrast, inorganic binders are made from mineral-based compounds like clay and silica, providing excellent thermal stability and resistance to high temperatures. Both types of binders play a crucial role in the foundry industry by bonding sand particles together to create molds for metal casting processes.</p></p>
