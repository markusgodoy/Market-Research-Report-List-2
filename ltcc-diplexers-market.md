<p><p>LTCC (Low Temperature Co-fired Ceramic) Diplexers are used in high frequency and low frequency applications within the RF and microwave industries. High frequency diplexers are designed to operate at frequencies generally above 1 GHz, while low frequency diplexers are optimized for frequencies below 1 GHz. Both types of diplexers are crucial for filtering and separating different frequency signals in communication systems. High frequency diplexers are commonly used in wireless communication devices, while low frequency diplexers are often utilized in radar systems and satellite communications.</p></p>
