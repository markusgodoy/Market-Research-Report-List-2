<p><p>Tension load cells are devices used to measure the force or tension applied to an object. There are several types of tension load cells available in the market, including piezoelectric load cells, hydraulic load cells, pneumatic load cells, and other types. Piezoelectric load cells utilize the piezoelectric effect to measure force, hydraulic load cells use hydraulic fluid, and pneumatic load cells use air pressure. Other types may include strain gauge load cells or capacitive load cells, each with their unique method of force measurement.</p></p>
