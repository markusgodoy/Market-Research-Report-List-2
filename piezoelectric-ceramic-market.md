<p><p>Piezoelectric ceramic market comprises various types of materials including Lead Zinc Titanates(PZT), Lead Titanate(PT), Lead Magnesium Niobate(PMN), and others. PZT is widely used due to its high piezoelectric properties and stability. PT is known for its high electromechanical coupling coefficients. PMN exhibits high piezoelectric properties at relatively low electric fields. Other types of piezoelectric ceramics offer unique properties suitable for specific applications. Overall, these different types cater to diverse needs in industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
