<p><strong>The Drip Irrigation Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Drip Irrigation</li><li>Subsurface Drip Irrigation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface drip irrigation involves laying drip irrigation pipes on the soil surface. This type of system is suitable for row crops and can be easily installed and maintained. Subsurface drip irrigation involves burying the drip irrigation pipes underground, delivering water directly to the plant root zone. This method minimizes water evaporation and reduces weed growth. Both surface and subsurface drip irrigation systems are efficient ways to deliver water to crops while conserving water resources and promoting healthier plant growth.</p></p>
