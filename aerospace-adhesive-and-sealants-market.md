<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aerospace adhesive and sealants market can be categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based adhesives and sealants are environmentally friendly, non-toxic, and have low VOC emissions. They are preferred for their easy clean-up and safe handling. On the other hand, solvent-based adhesives and sealants are known for their strong bonding capabilities and faster curing times. However, they contain volatile organic compounds that may be harmful to the environment and human health. Each type offers distinct advantages and is used in various aerospace applications.</p></p>
