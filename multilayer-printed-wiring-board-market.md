<p><p>Multilayer Printed-wiring Boards are categorized into three main types based on the number of layers they contain. Layer 4-6 boards have 4 to 6 layers of conductive material sandwiched between insulating layers, offering moderate complexity and functionality. Layer 8-10 boards contain 8 to 10 layers, providing increased complexity and capabilities for more advanced electronic devices. Layer 10+ boards have more than 10 layers, allowing for highly intricate designs and advanced functionality in complex electronic systems.</p></p>
