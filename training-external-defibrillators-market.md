<p><ul><li>Automatic</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Training external defibrillators come in two main types: Automatic and Manual. Automatic external defibrillators are designed to analyze the heart rhythm and deliver a shock if necessary without manual intervention. They are often used in public settings where quick and easy operation is crucial. Manual external defibrillators, on the other hand, are typically used by trained medical professionals who can manually adjust settings and administer shocks as needed. Both types are important in training scenarios to ensure individuals are prepared to use them effectively in real-life emergencies.</p></p>
