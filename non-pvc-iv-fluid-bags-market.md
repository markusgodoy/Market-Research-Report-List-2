<p><p>Non PVC IV fluid bags come in two market types: Single-chambered and Multi-chambered. Single-chambered bags contain a single compartment for storing fluids, making them suitable for simple infusions. Multi-chambered bags have multiple compartments separated by a barrier, allowing for the simultaneous storage and administration of different medications or fluids. They offer convenience and efficiency in delivering multiple therapies to patients. Both types of bags are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional PVC IV bags, reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
