<p><p>Polyurethane adhesives and sealants market types include water-based, solvent-based, hot-melt, and others. Hot-melt polyurethane adhesives are solid at room temperature and must be heated to activate. Other types may include moisture-curing polyurethanes or two-part systems. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the specific application needs.</p></p>
