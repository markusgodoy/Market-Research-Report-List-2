<p><p>Specialty Fiber Optic Cables for Electric Utilities and Communications are predominantly used in four market types: Optical Ground Wire (OPGW), Optical Phase Conductor (OPPC), All-Dielectric Self-Supporting (ADSS), and Optical Power Line Communication (OPLC). OPGW cables are integrated into transmission lines to provide fiber optic communication capabilities. OPPC cables serve a dual purpose of carrying electricity and transmitting data. ADSS cables are designed for aerial installation where no support or messenger wire is required. OPLC cables enable power line communication for smart grid applications.</p></p>
