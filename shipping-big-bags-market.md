<p><p>Shipping big bags come in two market types: contact seals and non-contact seals. Contact seals feature a zipper or other form of closure that securely seals the bag shut, preventing any contents from spilling out during transportation. Non-contact seals rely on the sheer size and weight of the bag to keep the contents secure, without the need for a physical seal. Both types offer different advantages depending on the specific needs and requirements of the user.</p></p>
