<p><p>Hydrogenated DCPD resin is a type of resin produced by hydrogenating dicyclopentadiene (DCPD) to improve its stability and performance. There are two main types of hydrogenated DCPD resin: fully hydrogenated DCPD and partially hydrogenated DCPD. Fully hydrogenated DCPD resin is completely saturated with hydrogen atoms, resulting in increased stability and resistance to oxidation. Partially hydrogenated DCPD resin has some unsaturated bonds remaining, providing a balance between flexibility and stability. Both types of resin are widely used in various industries for their unique properties.</p></p>
