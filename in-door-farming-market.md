<p><p>In-door farming includes various market types such as hydroponics, aeroponics, aquaponics, soil-based, hybrid, and others. Hydroponics involves growing plants in a nutrient-rich water solution, while aeroponics involves misting plant roots with nutrient solution. Aquaponics combines aquaculture and hydroponics, utilizing fish waste to fertilize plants. Soil-based farming is traditional farming method using soil as a growth medium. Hybrid farming combines different indoor farming techniques. Other market types may include vertical farming, container farming, and controlled environment agriculture.</p></p>
