<p><strong>The Hot Runner for Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Gate</li><li>Valve Gate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot runner systems are commonly used in injection molding for packaging applications. There are two main types of hot runner systems: open gate and valve gate. Open gate systems have a constant flow of molten plastic through the gate, allowing for faster cycle times and easier maintenance. Valve gate systems use a mechanical valve to control the flow of plastic, providing more precise control over the injection process and minimizing material waste. Each type has its advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the packaging application.</p></p>
