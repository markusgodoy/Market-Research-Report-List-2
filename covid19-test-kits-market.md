<p><p>COVID-19 test kits can be categorized into two main types - Nucleic Acid Test Kits and Antibody Test Kits. Nucleic Acid Test Kits are used to detect the genetic material of the virus in respiratory samples, providing accurate results to confirm an active infection. Antibody Test Kits, on the other hand, detect the presence of antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus, indicating past infection or potential immunity. Both types of test kits play a crucial role in controlling the spread of the virus and managing the pandemic effectively.</p></p>
