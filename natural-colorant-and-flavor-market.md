Chlorophyll is a green pigment found in plants, while polyphenols are antioxidants found in sources like berries and tea. Other natural colorants and flavors can include sources such as annatto and saffron. These natural ingredients provide vibrant colors and unique flavors to various food and beverage products.</p></p>
