<p><p>Engine antifreeze market is categorized into three main types: ethylene glycol, propylene glycol, and other types. Ethylene glycol is the most commonly used type due to its effective freezing point depression properties. Propylene glycol is preferred for its non-toxic and environmentally friendly characteristics. Other types of antifreeze may include mixtures of organic acid technology (OAT), hybrid organic acid technology (HOAT), or inorganic additives. Each type offers different benefits and applications, catering to a wide range of industries and vehicles.</p></p>
