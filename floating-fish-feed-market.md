<p><ul><li>Dry Floating Fish Feed</li><li>Wet Floating Fish Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floating fish feed market can be categorized into two main types: dry floating fish feed and wet floating fish feed. Dry floating fish feed is produced by extruding raw materials using high temperatures and pressures, resulting in a hard pellet that floats on water. Wet floating fish feed, on the other hand, is produced by mixing raw materials with water to form a dough-like consistency that is then extruded into pellets. Both types of floating fish feed are commonly used to provide essential nutrients to fish in aquaculture operations.</p></p>
