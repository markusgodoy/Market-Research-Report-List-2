<p><strong>The Potable Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Thermal Infrared Detector</li><li>Passive Thermal Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active Thermal Infrared Detector market consists of sensors that emit infrared radiation and measure the reflection or emission of the target object. These detectors are commonly used in applications such as temperature measurement and motion detection. On the other hand, Passive Thermal Infrared Detector market includes sensors that detect infrared radiation emitted by objects without emitting any radiation themselves. These detectors are used in applications such as night vision imaging and security systems for their ability to detect heat signatures.</p></p>
