<p><p>Cold Room Panels are structural insulated panels used to construct insulated rooms or spaces to store perishable goods at controlled temperatures. Cold room panels are widely used in industries like food storage, pharmaceuticals, and chemical storage to maintain the desired temperature and humidity levels.</p><p>The Cold Room Panels Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.9% during the forecast period.
