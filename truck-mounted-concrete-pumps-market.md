<p><p>Truck mounted concrete pumps can be classified into different types based on boom lengths. Short boom pumps have a reach below 28 meters, making them suitable for small to medium construction projects. Middle boom pumps have a reach of 28-47 meters, making them ideal for medium to large construction sites. Long boom pumps have a reach of 48-62 meters, offering excellent flexibility for high-rise construction projects. The "Others" category includes pumps with boom lengths exceeding 62 meters, catering to specialized and large-scale construction projects.</p></p>
