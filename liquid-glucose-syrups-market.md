<p><p>Liquid Glucose Syrups market types are classified based on their dextrose equivalent (DE) levels. Low conversion syrups with 20-35 DE are less sweet and used in products like dairy and confectionery. Intermediate conversion syrups with 35-55 DE are more versatile and commonly used in beverages and baked goods. High conversion syrups with 55-70 DE are very sweet and used in candies and desserts. Very high conversion syrups with 70-98 DE are extremely sweet and primarily used in pharmaceuticals and specialty foods.</p></p>
