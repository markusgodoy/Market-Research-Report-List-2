<p><p>Automatic lighting control sensors are devices that detect ambient light levels or motion to automatically control lighting in various spaces. There are two main types of sensors in the market: photocell sensors and passive infrared sensors. Photocell sensors work by detecting changes in light levels and adjusting lighting accordingly. Passive infrared sensors, on the other hand, detect motion and activate lighting when movement is detected. Both types of sensors are commonly used in commercial and residential buildings to improve energy efficiency and enhance user comfort.</p></p>
