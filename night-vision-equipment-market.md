<p><strong>The Night Vision Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Night vision equipment can be categorized into two main types: image intensifier and thermal image technology. Image intensifiers work by amplifying available light in order to produce a visible image in low-light conditions. On the other hand, thermal imaging technology detects heat signatures to create images in total darkness or through obstacles like smoke or fog. Both image intensifiers and thermal imaging systems are widely used in military, law enforcement, surveillance, and hunting applications due to their ability to provide enhanced visibility in challenging environments.</p></p>
