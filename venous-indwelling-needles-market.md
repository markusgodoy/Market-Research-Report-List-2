<p><ul><li>Open Indwelling Needle</li><li>Closed Indwelling Needle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Venous indwelling needles are available in two main types: open and closed indwelling needles. Open indwelling needles have a plastic hub that allows for easy access to the vein for blood drawing or medication administration. Closed indwelling needles, on the other hand, have a protective sheath that covers the needle when not in use, reducing the risk of needlestick injuries and contamination. Both types of indwelling needles are essential tools in healthcare settings for intravenous therapy and blood collection procedures.</p></p>
