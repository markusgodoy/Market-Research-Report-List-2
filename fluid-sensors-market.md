<p><p>Fluid sensors are devices used to measure the flow and level of liquids and gases in various industries. There are two main types of fluid sensors in the market: contact and non-contact. Contact sensors come into direct physical contact with the fluid to measure its properties, while non-contact sensors use technologies like ultrasonic or optical detection to measure without touching the fluid. Contact sensors are commonly used for monitoring fluid levels in tanks, while non-contact sensors are more suited for applications where contamination or damage is a concern.</p></p>
