<p><p>Agriculture utility adjuvants market types include compatibility agents, buffers/acidifiers, antifoam agents, drift control agents, and others. Compatibility agents ensure different components in the pesticide formulation work together effectively. Buffers/acidifiers help stabilize the pH level of the spray solution. Antifoam agents prevent excess foam formation during mixing and application. Drift control agents reduce off-target movement of pesticides. Other adjuvants may include wetting agents, spreaders, and sticking agents for improving pesticide efficacy and application efficiency in agriculture.</p></p>
