<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal</li><li>Thermal Transfer</li><li>Automatic</li><li>Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory printers and labelers can be categorized into different types based on their printing technology and operation. Direct thermal printers utilize heat to create images on labels, while thermal transfer printers use ribbons to transfer ink onto labels. Automatic printers are equipped with sensors and software to automate the printing process. Matrix printers use an array of pins to create images by striking an ink ribbon against the label. Each type offers unique benefits and functionalities to cater to different laboratory printing needs.</p></p>
