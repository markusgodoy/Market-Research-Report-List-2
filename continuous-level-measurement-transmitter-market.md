<p><p>Continuous Level Measurement Transmitters are devices used to measure the level of a substance in a tank or vessel. There are various types of level measurement transmitters available in the market, including Differential Pressure, Guided Wave Radar, Non-contact Radar, Ultrasonic, Capacitance, and others. Differential Pressure transmitters use pressure difference to calculate liquid level, Guided Wave Radar sends electromagnetic waves to determine level, Non-contact Radar uses radar technology, Ultrasonic measures level using sound waves, and Capacitance transmitters utilize changes in capacitance. Other types include laser and microwave technologies.</p></p>
