<p><p>Industrial pearlescent pigment market types include natural mica-based and synthetic mica-based pigments. Natural mica-based pigments are derived from minerals and offer a more sustainable and eco-friendly option compared to synthetic mica-based pigments, which are manufactured in a controlled environment. Natural mica-based pigments provide a unique shimmer effect while synthetic mica-based pigments offer more consistency in color and performance. Both types of pigments are commonly used in various industrial applications such as cosmetics, automotive coatings, plastics, and printing inks.</p></p>
