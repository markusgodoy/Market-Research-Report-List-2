<p><p>Intermittent Pneumatic Compression (IPC) System market is divided into two main types: IPC Garment and IPC Devices. IPC Garments are specifically designed sleeves or wraps that are worn on the limbs to apply intermittent pressure, promoting circulation and reducing swelling. IPC Devices, on the other hand, are machines that deliver compressed air to the garments, creating a cyclical pressure that helps improve blood flow. Both types of products are used in medical settings to prevent deep vein thrombosis, lymphedema, and other circulatory conditions.</p></p>
