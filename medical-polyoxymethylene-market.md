<p><p>Medical Polyoxymethylene, or POM, is a type of engineering plastic used in various medical applications due to its high strength, stiffness, and chemical resistance. There are two types of POM commonly used in the medical industry: homopolymer POM and copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM is a more rigid and stronger material, while copolymer POM offers improved impact resistance and flexibility. Both types of POM have their own unique characteristics that make them suitable for different medical devices and applications.</p></p>
