<p><p>Organic Caramel Color market is segmented into four classes based on the production process and application. Class I Caramel Color is produced by heating carbohydrates with acids or alkalis and is used in beverages, sauces, and baked goods. Class II Caramel Color is made by heating invert sugar and is used in meats and soy sauces. Class III Caramel Color is produced by heating ammonia and is used in beer and snacks. Class IV Caramel Color is made by heating sulfites and is used in pet foods and gravies.</p></p>
