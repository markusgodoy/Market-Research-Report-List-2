<p><p>MDI-based PU Soles (Footwear Polyurethane) market types refer to the different types of raw materials used in the production of polyurethane soles for footwear. MDI is known for its high performance and durability, while TDI is commonly used in lower grade footwear. Polyols are essential in the production process as they react with the isocyanates to form the polyurethane material. Each market type caters to different consumer needs and budget ranges.</p></p>
