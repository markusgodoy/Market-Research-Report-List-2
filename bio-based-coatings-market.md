<p><p>Bio-based coatings refer to coatings derived from natural materials that offer environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional coatings. The market for bio-based coatings can be classified into three main types: electrocoating technologies, primer surface technologies, and top coat technologies. Electrocoating technologies involve applying a protective coating to a conductive surface through an electric current. Primer surface technologies are used to prepare surfaces for coating adhesion. Top coat technologies provide the final layer of protection and aesthetic appeal to a coated surface.</p></p>
