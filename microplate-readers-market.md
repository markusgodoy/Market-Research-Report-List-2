<p><ul><li>Optical Filter Microplate Reader</li><li>Optical Grating Microplate Reader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microplate Readers are commonly used in various research labs and clinical settings for measuring absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence of samples in microplates. There are two main types of Microplate Readers - Optical Filter Microplate Reader and Optical Grating Microplate Reader. Optical Filter Microplate Readers use a set of fixed filters to select specific wavelengths of light, while Optical Grating Microplate Readers use diffraction gratings to produce a spectrum of light wavelengths. Both types offer high sensitivity and accuracy in measuring samples in microplates.</p></p>
