<p><p>Wireless antennas are crucial components in automotive communication systems. UHF antennas operate at higher frequencies, typically above 300 MHz, providing better signal quality and range for applications like GPS and Bluetooth. VHF antennas, on the other hand, operate at lower frequencies, typically between 30-300 MHz, and are commonly used for AM/FM radio reception in vehicles. Both types of antennas play a vital role in ensuring seamless wireless connectivity in automotive environments, enhancing driver experience and safety.</p></p>
