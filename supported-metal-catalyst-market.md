<p><ul><li>Single Metal Catalyst</li><li>Multimetallic Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Supported metal catalysts consist of metal nanoparticles dispersed on a solid support. Single metal catalysts contain only one type of metal, offering specific catalytic properties. Multimetallic catalysts, on the other hand, contain a combination of two or more metals, providing enhanced catalytic activity and selectivity. Single metal catalysts are widely used in various industrial processes, while multimetallic catalysts are gaining popularity for their synergistic effects and improved performance in complex reactions. Both types play a crucial role in catalysis and have distinct advantages depending on the specific application.</p></p>
