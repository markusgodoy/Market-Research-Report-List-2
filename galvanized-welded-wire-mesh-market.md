<p><p>Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh is available in two main types: Hot-dip Galvanized and Cold Galvanized. Hot-dip Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh is coated with a layer of molten zinc, providing superior corrosion resistance and durability, making it suitable for outdoor applications. On the other hand, Cold Galvanized Welded Wire Mesh is coated with a zinc-rich paint, offering a more cost-effective option for indoor or less demanding environments. Both types provide protection against rust and degradation, but Hot-dip Galvanized is typically preferred for heavy-duty or outdoor use.</p></p>
