<p><ul><li>Prime Lenses</li><li>Zoom Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Cine Lenses market consists of two main types: Prime Lenses and Zoom Lenses. Prime Lenses have a fixed focal length, providing a single, specific field of view. They are known for their superior image quality and wider apertures. On the other hand, Zoom Lenses offer variable focal lengths, allowing filmmakers to adjust the composition without changing lenses. They offer versatility and convenience, making them a popular choice for many cinematographers. Both types play a crucial role in capturing high-quality images in the digital cinema industry.</p></p>
