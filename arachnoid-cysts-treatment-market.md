<p><ul><li>Permanent Drainage System Surgery</li><li>Endoscopic Cyst Fenestration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent Drainage System Surgery is a type of treatment for arachnoid cysts where a catheter is surgically implanted to drain the cyst fluid into another body cavity. Endoscopic Cyst Fenestration is another type of treatment where an endoscope is used to create a small opening in the cyst wall to allow the fluid to drain. Both of these treatments are minimally invasive and aim to reduce symptoms such as headaches, seizures, and vision problems associated with arachnoid cysts.</p></p>
