<p><strong>The Fibre Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic sensors can be categorized into two main types: intrinsic and extrinsic sensors. Intrinsic sensors are directly integrated into the structure being monitored, providing accurate and real-time data on parameters such as strain, temperature, and pressure. Extrinsic sensors, on the other hand, are separated from the structure and are used to measure external environmental factors. Both types of fiber optic sensors have a wide range of applications in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and healthcare due to their high sensitivity, durability, and reliability.</p></p>
