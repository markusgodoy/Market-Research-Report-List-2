<p><p>LNMO Battery Materials Market Types include Electrode Sheets and Electrode Powder. Electrode Sheets are used as the base material for the production of electrodes in lithium nickel manganese oxide batteries, providing structural support and conducting electricity. Electrode Powder, on the other hand, consists of fine particles of lithium, nickel, manganese, and oxygen compounds that are mixed together to create the active material in the electrodes. Both types play a crucial role in the performance and efficiency of LNMO batteries.</p></p>
