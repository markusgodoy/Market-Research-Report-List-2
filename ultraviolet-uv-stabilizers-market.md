<p><p>Ultraviolet (UV) stabilizers market types include Hindered Amine Light Stabilizer (HALS), UV Absorbers, Quenchers, and other additives. HALS are antioxidant stabilizers that inhibit the degradation of polymers caused by UV radiation. UV Absorbers absorb harmful UV rays and convert them into harmless heat. Quenchers are free radical scavengers that prevent chain scission in polymers caused by UV exposure. Other additives in the market include light stabilizers, photoinitiators, and radical scavengers, which offer protection against UV-induced degradation in various polymer applications.</p></p>
