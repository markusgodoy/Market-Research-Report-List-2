<p><p>Integral waterproofing compound market is divided into two main types: liquid-based and powder-based. Liquid-based compounds are easy to mix with water and provide instant waterproofing properties. They are suitable for concrete surfaces and are often preferred for their ease of application. On the other hand, powder-based compounds are more concentrated and generally offer better waterproofing properties. They are typically used in demanding applications where a higher level of protection is required. Both types of compounds play a crucial role in preventing water damage and increasing the durability of structures.</p></p>
