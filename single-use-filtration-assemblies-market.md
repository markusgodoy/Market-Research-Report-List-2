<p><ul><li>Membrane Filtration Assemblies</li><li>Depth Filtration Assemblies</li><li>Centrifugation Assemblies</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-use filtration assemblies are used in various industries for efficient liquid filtration processes. Membrane filtration assemblies utilize a semipermeable membrane to separate particles from liquids. Depth filtration assemblies use a thick material to trap particles within the filter. Centrifugation assemblies utilize high-speed spinning to separate particles based on density. Other types of single-use filtration assemblies include vacuum filtration and gravity filtration. These different market types provide a range of options for industries needing efficient and convenient filtration solutions.</p></p>
