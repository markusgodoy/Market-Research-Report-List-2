<p><p>Fixed-mount barcode readers are commonly used in various industries for fast and accurate scanning of barcodes. There are three main types of fixed-mount barcode readers: laser scanners, linear imagers, and 2D imagers.</p><p>Laser scanners use a laser beam to read barcodes, offering high-speed scanning capabilities. Linear imagers capture barcode information by taking a picture of the barcode, while 2D imagers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes, providing more versatile scanning options. Each type of fixed-mount barcode reader is suited for different applications based on scanning speed, accuracy, and versatility.</p></p>
