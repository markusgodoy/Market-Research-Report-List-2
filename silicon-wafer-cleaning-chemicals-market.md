<p><p>The Silicon Wafer Cleaning Chemicals Market includes two main types of cleaning methods: Solvent Cleaning and Hydrofluoric Acid Dip. </p><p>Solvent cleaning involves using organic solvents to remove contaminants from the silicon wafer surface. This method is effective for removing particles and organic residues.</p><p>Hydrofluoric Acid Dip, on the other hand, is used to remove metallic and inorganic contaminants from the wafer surface. It is a more aggressive cleaning method that is particularly effective for etching and removing oxides from the wafer surface.</p></p>
