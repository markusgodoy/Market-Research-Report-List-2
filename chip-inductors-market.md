<p><strong>The Chip Inductors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Winding Type</li><li>Laminated Type</li><li>Film Type</li><li>Weaving Type</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chip inductors are classified into various types based on their construction and design. Winding type chip inductors consist of a wire wound around a core material. Laminated type chip inductors use multiple layers of magnetic material sandwiched together. Film type chip inductors have a thin film of magnetic material on a substrate. Weaving type chip inductors use a weaving technique to form the inductor. Other types of chip inductors include multilayer and ceramic chip inductors, each with their unique design and applications.</p></p>
