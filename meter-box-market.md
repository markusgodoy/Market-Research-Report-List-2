<p><p>Meter boxes are essential for safely housing electrical meters in residential and commercial buildings. There are two main types of meter boxes: single-phase and polyphase. Single-phase meter boxes are designed for systems with a single alternating current, while polyphase meter boxes are used in systems with multiple phases of electricity. Each type of meter box is specifically tailored to suit the needs and requirements of different electrical systems, providing efficient and reliable protection for the meters housed within them.</p></p>
