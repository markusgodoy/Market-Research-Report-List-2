<p><p>Magnetic Media Degaussers are devices used to erase data from magnetic media such as hard drives and tapes. There are three main types of degaussers: coil degaussers, capacitive discharge degaussers, and permanent magnet degaussers. </p><p>Coil degaussers use a coil to generate a magnetic field that demagnetizes the media. Capacitive discharge degaussers discharge a high amount of energy through a coil to erase the data quickly. Permanent magnet degaussers use a powerful magnet to erase the data by applying a strong magnetic field.</p></p>
