<p><ul><li>Transparent Thin Film Electroluminescent Display</li><li>Non-Transparent Thin Film Electroluminescent Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electroluminescent displays can be categorized into two main types: Transparent Thin Film Electroluminescent Displays and Non-Transparent Thin Film Electroluminescent Displays. Transparent displays allow light to pass through, making them suitable for use in see-through applications like smart windows or heads-up displays. Non-transparent displays, on the other hand, do not allow light to pass through and are commonly used in devices like dashboards, signage, and indicators where visibility is not a concern. Both types offer unique advantages and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
