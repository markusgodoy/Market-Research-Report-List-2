<p><p>Polyvinyl Acetate (PVAc) dispersions are commonly used in the market in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based PVAc dispersions are environmentally friendly, easy to clean, and offer good adhesion properties. They are widely used in various applications such as paper coatings, adhesives, and sealants. On the other hand, solvent-based PVAc dispersions are known for their faster drying times and higher resistance to humidity and chemicals. They are commonly used in industrial coatings, wood adhesives, and automotive paints.</p></p>
