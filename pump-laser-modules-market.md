<p><ul><li>Single Mode Pump Lasers</li><li>Multi-Mode Pump Lasers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pump laser modules are typically categorized into two main types: single mode pump lasers and multi-mode pump lasers. Single mode pump lasers are designed for applications that require high power and narrow beam divergence, making them ideal for telecommunications and high precision industrial processes. On the other hand, multi-mode pump lasers are more cost-effective and suitable for applications that do not require as much precision, such as materials processing and medical systems. Both types of pump lasers play a crucial role in various industries by providing efficient and reliable laser power sources.</p></p>
