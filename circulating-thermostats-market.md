<p><p>Circulating thermostats are commonly used in laboratories for precise temperature control in various applications. There are four main types of circulating thermostats in the market: benchtop, immersion, compact, and floor-standing. </p><p>Benchtop circulating thermostats are compact and easily placed on lab benches for small-scale applications. Immersion circulating thermostats are designed to be submerged in a liquid for direct temperature control. Compact circulating thermostats are small and portable, ideal for limited space settings. Floor-standing circulating thermostats are larger units suited for high-capacity cooling and heating requirements.</p></p>
