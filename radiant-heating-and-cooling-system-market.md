<p><p>Radiant heating and cooling systems are gaining popularity in the market, with various types available to suit different needs. Embedded surface systems involve pipes or cables placed within the floor, walls, or ceiling, providing efficient heating or cooling. Thermally active building systems use building surfaces as a temperature control system. Capillary surface systems use a network of capillaries to control temperature. Radiant panels are commonly used in ceilings for efficient heating or cooling. Each of these systems offers energy-efficient solutions for maintaining indoor comfort.</p></p>
