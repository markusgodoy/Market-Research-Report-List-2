<p><p>Fermented dairy products are dairy items that have been processed through fermentation, typically using lactic acid bacteria. This process involves breaking down the lactose in milk into lactic acid, resulting in a tangy flavor and improved shelf life. Common fermented dairy products include yogurt, kefir, buttermilk, and sour cream.</p><p>The Fermented Dairy Products Market is experiencing significant growth, with an expected CAGR of 13.5% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the rising demand for functional foods, increasing awareness of the health benefits of probiotics, and the growing popularity of dairy products with added probiotic cultures.</p><p>Consumers are increasingly seeking out fermented dairy products for their gut health benefits, as probiotics have been linked to improved digestion and immunity. Manufacturers are also responding to this trend by introducing new product innovations, such as plant-based fermented dairy alternatives and flavored yogurt varieties.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing focus on natural and clean label products is driving the demand for organic and non-GMO fermented dairy products. Overall, the Fermented Dairy Products Market is projected to continue its growth trajectory in the coming years.</p></p>
