<p><strong>The Dual-curing Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV curing</li><li>Visible light-curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual-curing adhesives are commonly categorized into two main types: UV curing and visible light-curing adhesives. UV curing adhesives are activated by ultraviolet light, which triggers a rapid chemical reaction to cure the adhesive. On the other hand, visible light-curing adhesives require visible light to initiate the curing process. Both types offer fast and efficient curing capabilities, making them popular choices in various industries such as electronics, automotive, and medical devices. The market for these adhesives continues to grow as manufacturers seek more reliable and fast curing solutions for their assembly processes.</p></p>
