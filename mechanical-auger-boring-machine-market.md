<p><ul><li>Crawler</li><li>Hydraulic Walking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical auger boring machines come in two main types: Crawler and Hydraulic Walking. Crawler machines are equipped with tracks similar to those found on tanks, allowing them to move across rough terrain and maneuver easily. Hydraulic Walking machines, on the other hand, have hydraulic legs that lift the machine off the ground, enabling it to move forward. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific project requirements, such as soil conditions and space limitations.</p></p>
