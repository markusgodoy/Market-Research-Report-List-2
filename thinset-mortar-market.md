<p><p>Thinset mortar is a type of adhesive used for installing tiles and stones. Dry-set mortar is a powdered form that is mixed with water before application. Latex-portland cement mortar is a pre-mixed blend that includes latex additives for improved flexibility and strength. Dry-set mortar is commonly used for interior applications, while latex-portland cement mortar is preferred for exterior and high-moisture areas. Both types offer strong bonding properties and are essential for durable and long-lasting tile installations.</p></p>
