<p><strong>The Wired Water Leak Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Leak Detector</li><li>Active Leak Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive leak detectors are devices that constantly monitor for water leaks and sound an alarm when water is detected. These detectors do not require any power source and are usually placed in areas prone to leaks, such as under sinks or near water heaters. </p><p>Active leak detectors, on the other hand, are devices that actively shut off the water supply when a leak is detected. These detectors require power and are usually connected to a home's water main. They provide an added layer of protection by instantly stopping the flow of water in the event of a leak.</p></p>
