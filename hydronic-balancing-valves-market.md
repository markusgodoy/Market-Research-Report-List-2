<p><p>Hydronic balancing valves are essential components in HVAC systems to regulate the flow of hot or cold water, ensuring proper distribution throughout the system. The types of hydronic balancing valves include static balancing valves, which maintain a constant flow rate, dynamic balancing valves, which adjust flow rates based on system demand, and Pressure Independent Control Valves (PICV), which combine control and balancing functions in one valve. Each type serves a specific purpose in optimizing system performance and efficiency.</p></p>
