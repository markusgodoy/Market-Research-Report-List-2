<p><p>The 3D printer filaments market can be categorized into various types such as HIPS, PVA, PET (CEP), metal, and others. HIPS filaments offer high impact resistance and are commonly used for prototypes and models. PVA filaments are water-soluble and are primarily used as support material during printing complex structures. PET (CEP) filaments provide strength and durability for functional parts. Metal filaments allow for printing metallic objects. Other types include specialty filaments like wood-infused or flexible materials, offering unique printing capabilities.</p></p>
