<p><p>Cutback Bitumen is a type of bitumen where a petroleum distillate is added to reduce its viscosity. Cutback Bitumen is classified into three main categories based on their curing time: Rapid Curing (RC), Medium Curing (MC), and Slow Curing (SC). Rapid Curing Cutback Bitumen dries quickly and is ideal for cold weather conditions. Medium Curing Cutback Bitumen has a moderate drying time and is commonly used for road construction. Slow Curing Cutback Bitumen takes a longer time to cure and is suitable for warmer climates.</p></p>
