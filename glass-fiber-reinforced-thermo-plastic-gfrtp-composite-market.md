<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Thermo-Plastic (GFRTP) Composite market can be categorized into different types based on the form of glass fibers used. Roving Glass Fibers are continuous strands of glass fibers used for reinforcing composites. Chopped Glass Fibers are small, randomly oriented glass fiber strands typically used in injection molding and extrusion processes. Yarn Glass Fibers are twisted strands of glass fibers used in weaving applications. Other types may include mats, fabrics, and non-woven materials that also serve as reinforcement in GFRTP composites.</p></p>
