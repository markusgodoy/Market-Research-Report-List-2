<p><p>Lactic acid is a naturally occurring organic acid commonly found in dairy products and fermented foods. There are three main types of lactic acid on the market: L-lactic acid, D-lactic acid, and DL-lactic acid. L-lactic acid is the most common form and is produced during fermentation by bacteria. D-lactic acid is less common and is produced by some bacteria. DL-lactic acid is a mixture of both L and D forms and has various industrial applications in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
