<p><p>Examination chair mounted colposcopes come in two main types: electronic colposcopy and optical colposcopy. Electronic colposcopy utilizes digital imaging technology to capture high-resolution images of the cervix, allowing for magnification and accurate visual examination. Optical colposcopy, on the other hand, relies on traditional lenses to magnify and view the cervix. Both types of colposcopes are mounted on examination chairs for convenience and ease of use during gynecological exams. These tools are essential for detecting abnormalities in the cervix and diagnosing conditions such as cervical cancer.</p></p>
