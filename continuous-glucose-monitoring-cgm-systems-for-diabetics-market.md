<p><p>Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM) systems for diabetics are available in two main types - wearable and implantable. Wearable CGM systems are external devices that are worn on the body and continuously monitor glucose levels through a sensor inserted under the skin. On the other hand, implantable CGM systems are surgically implanted under the skin and provide continuous glucose monitoring without the need for an external device. Both types of systems provide valuable real-time data to help diabetics manage their blood sugar levels effectively.</p></p>
