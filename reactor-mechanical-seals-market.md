<p><p>Reactor mechanical seals are used to prevent leakage in the equipment. In the market, there are three main types of reactor mechanical seals: single end, double-sided, and multi-faceted. Single end seals have one seal face contacting the rotating shaft, while double-sided seals have two seal faces. Multi-faceted seals have multiple seal faces that can provide better sealing performance. Each type of seal has its own advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the reactor system.</p></p>
