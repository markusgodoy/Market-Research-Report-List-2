<p><strong>The Marine Data Buoy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drifting Buoy</li><li>Moored Buoy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine Data Buoy Market consists of two main types: Drifting Buoy and Moored Buoy. Drifting Buoys are mobile devices that collect oceanographic data as they drift along with ocean currents, providing real-time information on various parameters such as temperature, salinity, and currents. Moored Buoys, on the other hand, are stationary devices anchored to the seafloor, collecting data at specific locations over a longer period of time. Both types of buoys play a crucial role in monitoring and understanding ocean conditions for various applications including weather forecasting and climate research.</p></p>
