<p><p>Automotive ambiance lighting is categorized into four main types: footwell, door, dashboard, and center console lighting. Footwell lighting is installed in the floor area of the vehicle, improving visibility and aesthetics. Door lighting enhances the interior ambiance and provides illumination when entering or exiting the vehicle. Dashboard lighting is located on the instrument panel, providing functional and decorative lighting. Center console lighting illuminates the storage and control area between the front seats, enhancing the overall interior atmosphere and user experience.</p></p>
