<p><p>Vertical profiling systems are used to collect data on water quality and conditions at various depths in bodies of water. There are two main types of vertical profiling systems: fixed and buoy-based. Fixed systems are typically anchored to the seafloor and collect data continuously at set intervals. Buoy-based systems are deployed on floating platforms and can be moved to different locations as needed. Both types of systems provide valuable information for researchers, scientists, and environmental monitoring agencies to better understand and manage marine environments.</p></p>
