<p><p>Chemical indicators are essential tools used to monitor different sterilization processes in healthcare facilities. The types of chemical indicators include Steam, Dry Heat, Ethylene Oxide (EO), Hydrogen Peroxide, and Others. Steam indicators are used for autoclave sterilization, Dry Heat indicators for hot air sterilization, EO indicators for gas sterilization, and Hydrogen Peroxide indicators for vaporized hydrogen peroxide sterilization. Other types of chemical indicators are available for various specific sterilization methods to ensure equipment and instruments are effectively sterilized.</p></p>
