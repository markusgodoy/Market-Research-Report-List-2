<p><ul><li>Straight Stairlift</li><li>Curved Stairlift</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wheelchair platform stairlifts come in two main types: straight stairlifts and curved stairlifts. Straight stairlifts are designed for stairs that are straight without any curves or turns. They are more affordable and easier to install compared to curved stairlifts. On the other hand, curved stairlifts are custom-designed to fit stairs with curves, corners, or intermediate landings. They are more expensive and require professional installation to ensure a perfect fit. Both types of stairlifts are essential for providing accessibility and independence to wheelchair users in homes and public buildings.</p></p>
